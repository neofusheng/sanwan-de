❓ FAQ

Is it really free?
The EasyClaw Desktop Edition is completely free and open source. The Lobster runs on your own computer, and your data stays in your hands. The Enterprise Cloud Edition offers additional premium features and 7×24 hosting services.
How is it different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is a chat window. The Lobster is your personal AI assistant — it remembers you, executes tasks automatically, connects to your tools (calendar, email, social media), stays on standby 24/7, and can learn any new skill you teach it.
What kind of computer do I need?
Anything that can run a browser — a MacBook, a Windows laptop. No GPU required. AI inference runs in the cloud. Even a Raspberry Pi can run it.
Is my data safe?
Desktop Edition: the Lobster runs on your own machine. Data doesn't pass through any third-party servers. Enterprise Cloud: dedicated cloud instance, data isolation, enterprise-grade security.
Do skills cost extra?
The vast majority of Skills are completely free. The ClawHub community has 200+ free skills to install. Some premium creation skills (image/video generation etc.) are billed by usage.
How do I install new skills?
Browse the skill marketplace at ClawHub.com, click install on any skill — or just tell your Lobster "install the XX skill for me" and it will handle it automatically.
